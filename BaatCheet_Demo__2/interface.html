⚙️ Technical Backend Workflow

1. Entry Point
Message received via webhook.js. User history is retrieved from aiEngine.js.
2. Parallel Processing (Claude 3.5 Sonnet)
aiEngine.js triggers two calls: Empathy (for the reply) and Triage (for severity/intensity).
3. Categorization & Risk Assessment
The 4-layer AI categorizes the issue (Stress, Anxiety, etc.) and flags potential crises (CrisisFlag).
4. Matchmaking
match.js finds a listener tier (Peer/Counselor) based on AI scores and checks Firebase availability.
5. Session & Privacy
session.js creates a private audio room and notifies the listener with a 3-line brief.
